Benefits

One Free Call To Block All Lost Cards

Lost card reporting: No matter where you are, one call to our 24 hour helpline will ensure that all your lost cards are blocked. We will call all your issuers and notify them to cancel the cards you reported as lost.

Emergency Travel And Hotel Assistance

Lost card reporting: No matter where you are, one call to our 24 hour helpline will ensure that all your lost cards are blocked. We will call all your issuers and notify them to cancel the cards you reported as lost.

When travelling within India or abroad, if your cards go missing, we help you:

Pay your hotel bills: In case you are not in a position to pay having lost your cards, we will facilitate payment of your hotel bill anywhere in the world, so that your trip continues and you live worry free. Hotel bills are covered up to Rs. 160,000 abroad and up to Rs. 80,000 in India.

Replacement travel tickets: In case you lose your travel tickets along with your cards, we ensure that you are not left stranded away from home, by providing replacement travel tickets anywhere in the world, so that you can return home safely and worry free. Travel tickets are covered up to Rs. 160,000 abroad and up to Rs. 80,000 in India.

Emergency Cash Advance

To ensure that you are able to continue your work, even if your cash is lost or stolen along with your cards, we will facilitate an emergency cash advance of up to Rs. 20,000. Cash advance is provided within India only.

The emergency assistance is advanced interest free and is to be re-paid within 28 days.

For a list of cities, where Emergency Cash Advance in India is provided, click here

Banking Protection With F-Secure Internet Security

Your membership now provides a 1-year subscription of F-Secure Internet Security which provides comprehensive protection while surfing, shopping, banking and using social media on your laptop or PC. Your net-banking and online e-commerce transactions are protected by an additional layer of security with the Banking Protection feature. F-Secure also protects your children against harmful, unsavory websites and keeps your private information and credentials concealed

Unlimited vHealth by Aetna

This benefit offers on demand access to qualified doctors with unlimited consultation. One can consult with in-house doctors by video or by voice call and avail teleconsultation for treatment of any illnesses, advice on alternative treatment options and interpretation of diagnostic reports.

Music Subscription

Get a 12-months subscription to Gaana Plus and listen to your favorite music Ad free on your favorite devices.

ZEE5 All Access Annual Subscription

This Product comes with a 12 month LIVE TV Subscription, which allows You to watch TV Shows, Originals, Movies, Music and News and much more, on a device of Your choice. This service is provided by ZEE.

Roadside Assistance

Your Card Protection membership offers you roadside assistance service for your car/bike, including services such as vehicle towing, flat – tyre support, battery jumpstart, fuel delivery among others. These services are available in 400+ cities across India, within 75 km from city center.

For List of cities, where services are provided, click here

Credit Health Report

Get a detailed analysis of your current credit score and history with Credit Health Report, provided by Credit Mantri. The CHR provides you with actionable insights to improve/ maintain your credit health by helping you understand the credit Information Report issued by Bureaus.

Free Replacement Of Lost PAN Card

PAN Cards are nowadays carried everywhere and serve as an important KYC role. They are often lost with the wallet. To ensure that you are able to live worry-free, we will help replace your lost PAN Card free of cost.

Complimentary Fraud Protection including Mobile Wallet Protection*

You will be enrolled into a complimentary add – on benefit of fraud protection within 7 days of your membership start date under the group policy taken by us for our customers, which ensures protection of your cards (up to 30 days prior to loss report) against frauds such as PIN Based, online, phishing, tele-phishing and transactions where OTP is not required (if card if lost/stolen) for up to Rs. 300,000. Your mobile wallets are also protected against frauds for up to Rs. 100,000 (if there is a simultaneous physical loss of device).

*You will enrolled into complimentary fraud protection benefit within seven (7) days of your membership start date. Fraud protection cover is provided by an Indian General Insurance Company registered with IRDAI under the group insurance policy for CPP members. Please refer to group policy terms & conditions for more details.
